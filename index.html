03 / GOVERNANCE

Holders decide.
Not founders.

PHASE 01
Off-chain voting

Before Raydium graduation, governance operates through Snapshot. ASX holders connect wallets. Voting weight is proportional to holdings. All proposals and results published publicly.

PHASE 02
On-chain governance

After graduating to Raydium, Astryx deploys through Realms — Solana's native on-chain governance protocol. Proposals, votes, and outcomes recorded permanently on-chain. Trustless by design.

WHAT HOLDERS VOTE ON
Real decisions

Project roadmap and development priorities. Use of the community treasury. Any significant structural changes to Astryx. The first DAO vote after graduation decides the roadmap itself.

PROPOSAL PROCESS
Open to all holders

Any holder can submit a proposal. A minimum discussion period precedes voting. Simple majority to pass. Passed proposals are binding and executed publicly. No founder veto.
